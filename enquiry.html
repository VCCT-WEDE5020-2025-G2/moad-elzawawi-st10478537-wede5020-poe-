Volunteer Opportunities

Our volunteers are the heart of our organization. We offer flexible opportunities that match your skills, interests, and availability.

Educational Support Volunteers

After-School Tutoring
  • Time Commitment: 2-4 hours per week
  • Schedule: Monday-Thursday, 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM
  • Requirements: Grade 12 minimum, patient and encouraging
  • Subjects: Mathematics, English, Science, Life Skills
Reading Programme Assistant
  • Time Commitment: 2 hours per week
  • Schedule: Flexible morning or afternoon slots
  • Requirements: Love of reading, multilingual preferred
  • Activities: One-on-one reading, storytelling
Computer Literacy Instructor
  • Time Commitment: 3 hours per week
  • Schedule: Saturdays 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • Requirements: Basic IT skills, teaching ability
  • Focus: Digital literacy for students and parents

Feeding Programme Volunteers

Meal Preparation
  • Time Commitment: 4 hours per session
  • Schedule: Monday-Friday, 5:30 AM - 9:30 AM
  • Requirements: Food safety certification (provided)
  • Tasks: Cooking, packing, basic kitchen duties
Food Distribution
  • Time Commitment: 2 hours per day
  • Schedule: Various school visit times
  • Requirements: Reliable transport, friendly demeanor
  • Activities: Delivering meals, interacting with children

Community Events & Administration

Event Organization
  • Time Commitment: 8-16 hours per event
  • Schedule: Flexible, based on event calendar
  • Requirements: Organization skills, team player
  • Tasks: Setup, registration, crowd management
Administrative Support
  • Time Commitment: 4-8 hours per week
  • Schedule: Office hours or remote work
  • Requirements: Computer skills, attention to detail
  • Tasks: Data entry, communications, filing
Social Media & Marketing
  • Time Commitment: 3-5 hours per week
  • Schedule: Flexible, remote friendly
  • Requirements: Social media experience, creativity
  • Activities: Content creation, photography, outreach

Specialized Skills Volunteers

Garden Mentors
  • Time Commitment: 4 hours per week
  • Schedule: Weekends preferred
  • Requirements: Gardening knowledge, patience
  • Focus: Teaching sustainable growing practices
Professional Mentors
  • Time Commitment: 2 hours per month
  • Schedule: Flexible meeting times
  • Requirements: Professional experience in any field
  • Activities: Career guidance, university prep
Skills Workshop Facilitators
  • Time Commitment: 6 hours per workshop
  • Schedule: Monthly workshops
  • Requirements: Expertise in specific skill areas
  • Topics: Financial literacy, entrepreneurship, parenting

Corporate Sponsorship & Partnership Opportunities

Partner with Cape Hope Foundation to make a meaningful impact while showcasing your organization's commitment to social responsibility.

Programme Sponsor

R50,000 - R100,000 annually

Benefits Include:
  • Sponsor an entire programme (feeding scheme, tutoring, etc.)
  • Logo placement on programme materials
  • Quarterly impact reports
  • Employee volunteer opportunities
  • Annual recognition event invitation
  • Social media recognition

Impact: Directly supports 100-500 children annually

School Partner

R25,000 - R50,000 annually

Benefits Include:
  • Sponsor all programmes at one partner school
  • Company name recognition at school
  • Bi-annual school visit opportunities
  • Progress reports on sponsored students
  • Employee mentorship programmes
  • Website and newsletter recognition

Impact: Supports 150-300 children at one school

Community Champion

R10,000 - R25,000 annually

Benefits Include:
  • Support specific community events or workshops
  • Logo placement at sponsored events
  • Employee volunteer opportunities
  • Annual impact summary
  • Newsletter recognition
  • Event photo opportunities

Impact: Reaches 50-100 families through events
