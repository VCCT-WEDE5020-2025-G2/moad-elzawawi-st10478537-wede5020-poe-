About Cape Hope Foundation

Discover our story, mission, and the passionate team dedicated to empowering Cape Town's youth

Our Story

Cape Hope Foundation was established in 2015 by a group of passionate educators and community leaders who recognized the urgent need for comprehensive support for underprivileged children in Cape Town's townships.

What started as a small feeding scheme at a single primary school has grown into a comprehensive support network spanning 15 schools across Cape Town, directly impacting thousands of children and their families.

Our journey began when founder Sarah Mitchell, a former teacher, witnessed firsthand how hunger and lack of educational resources prevented talented children from reaching their potential. Together with local community leaders, she established Cape Hope Foundation with a simple but powerful vision: every child deserves the opportunity to learn, grow, and thrive.

Key Milestones

  • 2015: Foundation established, first feeding scheme launched at Khanya Primary School
  • 2017: After-school tutoring programme introduced
  • 2019: Expanded to serve 5 schools across Cape Town
  • 2021: Launched community garden project and parent workshops
  • 2023: Reached milestone of serving 2,000 children daily
  • 2025: Celebrating 10 years of impact with 15 partner schools

Our Mission

To provide food security and educational support to underprivileged children, empowering them to break the cycle of poverty through education, community partnerships, and holistic development programmes.

Our Vision

A future where every child in Cape Town has access to quality education, nutritious meals, and the support needed to reach their full potential, regardless of their socio-economic background.

Our Values

  • Dignity: Treating every child with respect and recognizing their inherent worth
  • Community: Building strong partnerships with schools, families, and local organizations
  • Sustainability: Creating long-term solutions that empower communities
  • Transparency: Maintaining open communication with all stakeholders
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest quality in all our programmes

Success Stories

Thabo Mthembu

Programme Graduate 2024

Now studying Education at University of the Western Cape

Amahle Ndaba

Scholarship Recipient 2023

First in her family to attend university

Sipho Mgxekwa
